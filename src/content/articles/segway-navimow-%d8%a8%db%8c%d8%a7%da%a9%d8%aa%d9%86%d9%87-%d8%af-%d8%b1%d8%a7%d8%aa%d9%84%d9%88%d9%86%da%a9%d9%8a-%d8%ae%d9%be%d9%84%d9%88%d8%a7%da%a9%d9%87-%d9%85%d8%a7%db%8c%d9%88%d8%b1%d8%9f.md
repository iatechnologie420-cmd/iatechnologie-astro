<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="1_%D8%AF_%DA%A9%D8%A7%D8%B1%D9%88%D9%84%D9%88_%D8%A7%D8%B3%D8%A7%D9%86%D8%AA%DB%8C%D8%A7"></span>1. د کارولو اسانتیا<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>

<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="2_%D9%88%D8%AE%D8%AA_%D8%A7%D9%88_%D9%87%DA%85%DB%90_%D8%AE%D9%88%D9%86%D8%AF%D9%8A_%DA%A9%DA%93%D8%A6"></span>2. وخت او هڅې خوندي کړئ<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>

<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="3_%D8%AF%D9%82%DB%8C%D9%82_%D8%A7%D9%88_%DB%8C%D9%88%D9%86%DB%8C%D9%81%D9%88%D8%B1%D9%85_%DA%A9%D8%B1%D9%84"></span>3. دقیق او یونیفورم کرل<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>
