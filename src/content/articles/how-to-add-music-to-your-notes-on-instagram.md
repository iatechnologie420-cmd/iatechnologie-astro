<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Use_music_features_on_Instagram"></span>Use music features on Instagram<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<p>Instagram has become an essential platform for sharing life moments, images and videos. To make your posts even more engaging, you can integrate music into your content. In this article, we&#8217;ll explore the different music features on Instagram and how to use them to create original and engaging posts.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Why_add_music_to_your_stories"></span>Why add music to your stories<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<p>Adding music to your Instagram Stories can be a powerful way to improve your followers&#8217; experience and make your content more engaging. Music can help set a mood, stir emotions, and grab users&#8217; attention.</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Add_music_to_Stories"></span>Add music to Stories<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>Stories are short videos or images that disappear after 24 hours. To make them more dynamic, you can add music by following these steps:</p>



<p>1. Open the Instagram app and tap the camera icon at the top left of your screen.<br>2. Take a photo or record a video by tapping the circular button at the bottom of the screen.<br>3. Once your content is saved, you can tap the smiley face sticker at the top of the screen.<br>4. Select the “Music” option from the drop-down menu.<br>5. Search for the song you want to add using the built-in search engine.<br>6. Choose a sample of the song by adjusting the playing time.<br>7. Customize the appearance of the music sticker by choosing the size and location.<br>8. Tap “Done” to add the music to your Story.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Share_posts_with_music"></span>Share posts with music<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<p>Besides Stories, you can also add music to your permanent posts on Instagram. Here&#8217;s how to do it:</p>



<p>1. Open the Instagram app and tap the &#8220;+&#8221; icon at the bottom of the screen to create a new post.<br>2. Select the image or video you want to share from your gallery or by taking a new photo/video.<br>3. On the post editing screen, tap the musical note icon at the top of the screen.<br>4. Search for the song you want to add using the built-in search engine.<br>5. Select a sample of the song by adjusting the playing time.<br>6. Choose whether you want to display the song lyrics or the title and artist.<br>7. Customize the appearance of the music sticker by choosing the size and location.<br>8. When you&#8217;re done, tap &#8220;Share&#8221; to post your content with music.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Use_sound_effects_on_Instagram"></span>Use sound effects on Instagram<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<p>In addition to adding music, Instagram also offers sound effects that you can integrate into your Stories and posts. Here&#8217;s how to use them:</p>



<p>1. Follow the steps mentioned above to add content to your Story or post.<br>2. Instead of selecting &#8220;Music&#8221;, choose the &#8220;Sound Effects&#8221; option.<br>3. Browse the different categories of sound effects available (e.g. animals, city, nature, etc.).<br>4. Select the sound effect you want to add to your content.<br>5. Customize the appearance of the sound effect sticker by choosing the size and location.<br>6. Tap &#8220;Done&#8221; or &#8220;Share&#8221; to add the sound effect to your Story or post.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Tips_for_Adding_Music_Effectively"></span>Tips for Adding Music Effectively<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<p><strong>1. Adapt music to your content</strong></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="1_Adapt_music_to_your_content"></span><strong>1. Adapt music to your content</strong><span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>To maximize the impact of your music, choose a song that matches the mood or message you want to convey with your story. If you are sharing a happy moment, opt for upbeat and happy music. If you want to create a calm and soothing atmosphere, choose a softer, relaxing song.</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="2_Adjust_the_volume"></span><strong>2. Adjust the volume</strong><span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>Make sure to adjust the volume of the music so that it is neither too loud nor too quiet. It&#8217;s important that the music doesn&#8217;t drown out your voice or the sound of your video, but is audible enough to add ambiance to your content.</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="3_Experiment_with_music_stickers"></span><strong>3. Experiment with music stickers</strong><span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>Instagram also offers interactive music stickers to make your content even more engaging. You can have your followers tap on it to listen to the song in full, or set up a Q&#038;A feature related to the music you used.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="To_sum_up"></span>To sum up <span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>&#8211; Choose music that matches your message or the emotion you want to convey. Well-chosen music can enhance the impact of your content and elicit an emotional response from your audience.</p>



<p>&#8211; Make sure the volume of the music is balanced against the other audio elements in your post. If the music is too loud, it may drown out your message, while music that is too quiet may go unnoticed.</p>



<p>&#8211; Use transitions and sound effects to create an immersive audiovisual experience. Smooth transitions and well-placed sound effects can make your post more professional and engaging.</p>



<p>&#8211; Don&#8217;t forget to credit the artist or music source if you use a specific song in your post. This shows your respect for copyright and can also help promote the artist.</p>



<p>By incorporating music into your Instagram posts, you can significantly improve your followers&#8217; experience and make your content more memorable. Whether to accompany a story, highlight a video or simply add ambiance to a photo, music can be a powerful tool to reinforce your social media posts. </p>



<p>So don&#8217;t be afraid to experiment and find the pieces that best suit your style and message.</p>
