<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="%D8%AF_AI_%D9%84%D8%AE%D9%88%D8%A7_%D8%AF_%D8%A7%D9%86%D8%B3%D8%A7%D9%86%D9%8A_%D8%A7%D8%B3%D8%AA%D8%AE%D8%A8%D8%A7%D8%B1%D8%A7%D8%AA%D9%88_%D8%B3%D9%85%D9%88%D9%84%D8%9F"></span>د AI لخوا د انساني استخباراتو سمول؟<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="%D8%AF_%D8%A7%D9%86%D8%B3%D8%A7%D9%86_%D8%AF_%D8%A7%D8%B3%D8%AA%D8%AE%D8%A8%D8%A7%D8%B1%D8%A7%D8%AA%D9%88_%D8%B3%D9%85%D9%88%D9%84_%DA%85%D9%87_%D8%B4%DB%8C_%D8%AF%DB%8C%D8%9F"></span>د انسان د استخباراتو سمول څه شی دی؟<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>

<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="%DA%A9%D9%84%DB%8C%D8%AF%D9%8A_AI_%D9%BC%DB%8C%DA%A9%D9%86%D8%A7%D9%84%D9%88%DA%98%D9%8A"></span>کلیدي AI ټیکنالوژي<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>

<p>&#8211; <strong>د طبیعي ژبې پروسس کول (TLP)</strong> : ټیکنالوژي چې ماشینونو ته اجازه ورکوي چې د انسان ژبه درک او تولید کړي.</p>

<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="%D8%AF_%D8%A7%D9%86%D8%B3%D8%A7%D9%86_%D8%AF_%D9%87%D9%88%DA%9A%DB%8C%D8%A7%D8%B1%D8%AA%DB%8C%D8%A7_%D8%B3%D9%85%D8%A8%D9%88%D9%84%D9%88%D9%86%D9%88_%D8%B1%D9%88%DA%9A%D8%A7%D9%86%D9%87_%D9%85%D8%AB%D8%A7%D9%84%D9%88%D9%86%D9%87"></span>د انسان د هوښیارتیا سمبولونو روښانه مثالونه<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>
