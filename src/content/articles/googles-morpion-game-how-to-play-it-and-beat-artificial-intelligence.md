<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="The_rules_of_Googles_Tic-Toe_game"></span>The rules of Google&#8217;s Tic-Toe game<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Objective_of_the_game"></span>Objective of the game<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>The Morpion game, also called Tic-tac-toe, is a strategy game that is played on a 3&#215;3 grid. The goal is to line up three identical symbols (cross or circle) horizontally, vertically or diagonally before your opponent.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Set_up"></span>Set up<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>The Google Tic Toe game is available online and can be played on different devices, including smartphones, tablets or computers. To get started, simply go to the Google website and search for “Tic Toe game” in the search bar.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Game_progress"></span>Game progress<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Once on the game page, you can choose to play against an artificial intelligence, also known as Google AI, or against another player. If you choose to play against Google AI, you can select the difficulty level: easy, medium or hard.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Techniques_for_winning"></span>Techniques for winning<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>&#8211; Start by occupying the center of the grid: by starting from the center, you increase your chances of winning, because this square is the starting point for many possible alignments.</p>



<p>&#8211; Block opponent&#8217;s moves: Keep an eye on your opponent&#8217;s moves and try to block their potential lineups by placing your symbols in strategic locations.</p>



<p>&#8211; Anticipate next moves: try to predict your opponent&#8217;s moves and place your symbols to counter their strategies.</p>



<p>&#8211; Be flexible in your approach: don&#8217;t lock yourself into a single strategy, be ready to change tactics depending on your opponent&#8217;s moves.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Additional_Tips"></span>Additional Tips<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>&#8211; Don&#8217;t underestimate the &#8220;easy&#8221; level: even if you are an experienced player, the &#8220;easy&#8221; level can be good practice for testing new strategies or refining your game.</p>



<p>&#8211; Have fun: the Tic Toe game is a simple and fun game that can be played quickly. Take advantage of each game to have fun and improve your skills.</p>

<p><br><strong>1. Understanding the rules of the game:</strong><br>Before strategizing to beat the AI, it is essential to understand the rules of the Tic Toe game. Make sure you know the objectives, permitted actions and victory criteria. This will allow you to make informed decisions throughout the game.</p>



<p><strong>2. Observe the behavior of the AI:</strong><br>One of the first steps to beating the AI ​​is to observe it carefully. Note the movements she makes, the patterns she follows, and any mistakes she makes. This will give you clues about the strategies she uses and help you find ways to counter them.</p>



<p><strong>3. Create unexpected patterns:</strong><br>Once you understand the patterns of AI actions, you can use them to your advantage by creating unexpected patterns. For example, if the AI ​​tends to favor horizontal movements, try to trick it into making vertical or diagonal movements. This can disrupt his strategies and give him a hard time.</p>



<p><strong>4. Block AI victory opportunities:</strong><br>One of the key strategies for beating the AI ​​is to block its opportunities to win. If you see that the AI ​​is about to complete a row, column, or diagonal, place your symbol in a box that prevents it from doing so. This could force her to re-evaluate her options and take a less predictable approach.</p>



<p><strong>5. Anticipate future AI movements:</strong><br>To beat the AI, it is important to anticipate its future movements. Analyze game positions and try to predict where the AI ​​might place its next symbol. This will allow you to effectively block their strategies and gain the advantage by occupying key squares.</p>



<p><strong>6. Exploit your mistakes:</strong><br>Although AIs are generally very competent, they can sometimes make mistakes. If you spot a mistake, take this opportunity to counter it and gain an advantage. For example, if the AI ​​forgets to block your next winning line, take this opportunity to complete it and win the game.</p>



<p>By following these strategies, you will increase your chances of beating artificial intelligence at the game of Tic Toe. However, remember that AIs can also learn from their mistakes and adapt, so it&#8217;s important to continue evolving and refining your strategies throughout the game.</p>
