<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Incorporating_digital_technology_into_Hyundai_operations"></span>Incorporating digital technology into Hyundai operations<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<p>Hyundai has adopted various digital technologies to optimize its operations. For example, she adopted the <strong>Big Data</strong> and machine learning to analyze customer driving habits, optimize supply chain management and improve quality control of its vehicles. Additionally, the use of technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI), Internet of Things (IoT), and 3D printing has transformed all aspects of manufacturing, improving vehicle design, production , and testing.</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="The_role_of_innovative_digital_technologies_in_Hyundais_customer_experience"></span>The role of innovative digital technologies in Hyundai&#8217;s customer experience<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>At Hyundai, digital innovation is used to improve the customer experience. Indeed, the company has developed mobile applications allowing users to remotely control certain features of their cars, which brings a level of connectivity and personalization never before achieved. Virtual reality is also being used in showrooms to allow customers to create and visualize their ideal car.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Bringing_AI_to_the_automobile_Hyundais_decisive_role"></span>Bringing AI to the automobile: Hyundai&#8217;s decisive role<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>Artificial Intelligence (AI) is no longer a foreign concept in the automotive industry, and Hyundai is playing a crucial role in this regard. Hyundai AI vehicles offer innovative technologies like <strong>SmartSense</strong>, which includes a variety of advanced AI-assisted safety features. These include highway driving assistance, emergency braking assistance and blind spot detection.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Hyundai_and_the_Virtual_World_Virtual_Showroom_Experiences"></span>Hyundai and the Virtual World: Virtual Showroom Experiences<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>In an increasingly virtual world, Hyundai has been able to adapt and innovate. With the introduction of virtual showrooms, Hyundai has truly pushed the boundaries of the car buying experience. Offering an interactive tour of its vehicles, this virtual showroom allows customers to explore new vehicles from the comfort of their home, simulating real showroom interaction.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Hyundai_Drive_A_digital_approach_to_vehicle_maintenance"></span>Hyundai Drive: A digital approach to vehicle maintenance<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Hyundai is also outdoing itself by offering its customers a digital car service experience through its application <strong>Hyundai Drive</strong>. The app provides detailed vehicle maintenance information, allows booking appointments for maintenance services, and even gives a cost estimate, thereby providing a stress-free and conflict-free car service experience.</p>

<p>Hyundai clearly demonstrates its ambition to be a major player in electric cars through its models <strong>IONIQ 5 and IONIQ 6</strong>.
