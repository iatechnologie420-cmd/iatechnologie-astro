<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Mobile_operating_systems_market_shares"></span>Mobile operating systems market shares<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>With the rise of smartphones and the constant evolution of technology, the rivalry between <strong>iOS, Android and Windows</strong> becomes more and more intense. The market shares for each operating system vary inventively depending on several factors.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Mobile_operating_system_market_growth_forecast_in_2024"></span>Mobile operating system market growth forecast in 2024<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>According to recent reports <strong>smartphone sales</strong> have increased, driven by the growing adoption of <strong>5G and the new iPhone</strong>. This is thought-provoking about the mobile operating system growth forecast for 2024.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Android_gets_a_head_start"></span>Android gets a head start<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Between <strong>Android and iOS</strong>, competition is fierce. However, it seems that Android is taking a head start by becoming the number 2 mobile OS according to a Gartner analysis. It should be noted that in France, the success of the iPhone allowed Apple to grow by 4.2%. Furthermore, the meteoric rise of HarmonyOS makes it a potential competitor that could even supplant iOS in China.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="The_fall_of_Windows_Phone"></span>The fall of Windows Phone<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Concerning Windows, the situation is rather delicate. Windows Phone sales have fallen drastically, indicating a decline in Microsoft&#8217;s mobile operating system. Whether this is a disaster or a strategy remains a matter of debate among market analysts.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Impact_of_Apples_new_rules_on_advertising"></span>Impact of Apple&#8217;s new rules on advertising<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Beyond growth forecasts, Apple&#8217;s new policies have a considerable impact on the market. For example, recent changes to advertising regulations have seriously affected Criteo, a leading digital advertising company turned unicorn.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="ARM_processors_and_the_laptop_market"></span>ARM processors and the laptop market<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>The evolution of mobile operating systems is having a snowball effect on other segments of the technology market. ARM processors, commonly used in mobile devices, are predicted to account for 25% of the laptop market by 2028.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Samsung_dominates_the_global_smartphone_market"></span>Samsung dominates the global smartphone market<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>When it comes to the smartphone market, Samsung establishes itself as the global leader for the third quarter of 2023. This success strengthens the position of Android, the operating system on which most Samsung smartphones run.</p>

<p>2024 marks a new era for mobile operating systems. Being equipped with a good operating system has become an essential necessity to guarantee the security and efficiency of our devices. With <strong>iOS</strong>, <strong>Android</strong> And <strong>Windows</strong> as major players in the market, a comparative analysis of their market share is necessary. </p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="iOS_supremacy_on_the_market_at_the_end_of_2022_and_2023"></span>iOS supremacy on the market at the end of 2022 and 2023<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>At the end of 2022, the<strong>Apple iPhone</strong> achieved a feat by rising to first place in the global mobile operating system market. A remarkable performance that demonstrates the excellence of Apple technology and its ability to meet the needs of modern users.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Android_the_strength_of_accessibility"></span>Android, the strength of accessibility<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>The attraction of<strong>Android</strong> comes from its ability to offer a versatile operating system accessible on a variety of devices, its openness and compatibility with many mobile devices has allowed Android to maintain a significant market share over the years. </p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Windows_the_underestimated_potential"></span>Windows, the underestimated potential<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>The mobile operating system <strong>Windows</strong> has a smaller market share compared to iOS and Android. However, let&#8217;s not forget its capabilities, in particular its fluid integration with the Microsoft ecosystem, which could well allow it to surprise in the future. </p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Security_and_voice_assistants_the_new_fronts_of_competition"></span>Security and voice assistants, the new fronts of competition<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>The year 2024 also saw the advent of new trends, of which security and artificial intelligence are the highlights. Likewise, the <strong>voice assistants</strong> have established themselves as a new axis of competition between these technological giants, opening a new path towards the future of the human-machine interface.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Factors_influencing_the_market_shares_of_iOS_Android_and_Windows"></span>Factors influencing the market shares of iOS, Android and Windows<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<p>The mobile operating system market is multifactorial and constantly evolving. The market share between iOS, Android and Windows is largely influenced by several factors, including <strong>technological innovation</strong>, <strong>accessibility</strong>, <strong>AI integration</strong>, <strong>consumer preferences</strong>, L&#8217;<strong>application ecosystem</strong>, <strong>the price</strong>, among others.  </p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Market_share_forecasts_in_2024_iOS"></span>Market share forecasts in 2024: iOS<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Apple&#8217;s iOS has always been a leader in innovation and design. However, despite high-quality products, the high price of Apple devices is likely to hamper its growth in emerging markets. On the other hand, optimized AI integration could boost Apple&#8217;s market share.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Market_share_forecasts_in_2024_Android"></span>Market share forecasts in 2024: Android<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Android, due to its flexibility and accessibility, currently has a large market share. The variety of Android devices available at various prices has promoted its acceptance worldwide. This could be a long-term benefit for Google, but greater fragmentation could be both a blessing and a curse.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Market_share_forecasts_in_2024_Windows"></span>Market share forecasts in 2024: Windows<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Windows Mobile has long been an underdog in the operating system wars. However, Microsoft is investing heavily in research and development to regain its share of the pie. With good AI integration and an improved application ecosystem, Windows could surprise in 2024.</p>



<p>It is clear that the mobile operating system market will continue to evolve and adapt to new technologies and consumer preferences. It’s a delicate balancing act between continuous innovation, understanding consumers and adapting to market dynamics. Either way, it looks like 2024 will be another exciting year for the world of mobile operating systems.</p>
