<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Introduction_to_prompt_engineering"></span>Introduction to prompt engineering<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="What_is_Prompt_Engineering"></span>What is Prompt Engineering?<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>    THE <strong>Prompt Engineering</strong> is an emerging field that focuses on optimizing the prompts, or commands, we give to artificial intelligence (AI) systems, particularly those based on natural language, such as text generators. It is a particularly important discipline with the arrival of language processing models like GPT-4 from <strong>OpenAI</strong>. The idea is to learn to “talk” effectively to these AIs to improve the quality and relevance of the responses obtained.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Why_is_Prompt_Engineering_important"></span>Why is Prompt Engineering important?<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>The role of <strong>Prompt Engineering</strong> is crucial because the way you formulate a command to an AI can greatly vary the results. For example, poorly designed prompts could produce inaccurate or off-topic responses, while well-designed prompts can improve the accuracy and relevance of the information generated. Prompt engineering experts work to refine the wording of questions to obtain accurate and useful results.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="How_Prompt_Engineering_works"></span>How Prompt Engineering works<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p> The process of prompt engineering involves deeply understanding how AI models, such as neural networks, work and using that understanding to craft prompts that take advantage of the AI&#8217;s capabilities while working around its limitations. This can require some creativity, a lot of experimentation, and careful analysis of the results to iteratively refine the prompts.</p>



<p>The art of <strong>Prompt Engineering</strong> represents an essential skill for anyone seeking to interact effectively with the most advanced artificial intelligence systems. Understanding and applying the principles of prompt engineering can greatly improve the quality and effectiveness of our engagement with AI-based technologies.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Good_practice_and_technique_in_Prompt_Engineering"></span>Good practice and technique in Prompt Engineering<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>The art of prompt engineering for content and image generative AIs, like OpenAI and MidJourney, requires a combination of techniques and best practices to achieve quality results. Here are some of these best practices and techniques:</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Using examples:</strong> Incorporating sample requests and responses into your prompts can condition the model to respond in a desired manner, using one-shot or few-shot learning techniques to improve the accuracy of the model&#8217;s response.</li>



<li><strong>Pay attention to the clues:</strong> Including hints in your prompt can guide the model to generate output aligned with your intentions. This can be particularly useful in directing the model towards the desired response.</li>



<li><strong>Test different arrangements:</strong> The order in which information is presented in the prompt can influence the model output. It is helpful to experiment with different arrangements of instructions, main content, examples, and clues.</li>



<li><strong>Provide &#8220;output&#8221; to the model:</strong> Sometimes the model may have difficulty completing a task accurately. To mitigate this, provide alternative paths or instructions for the model to follow if it cannot find a satisfactory answer.</li>



<li><strong>Watch the length:</strong> Prompts may be subject to character limits. Prompts that are too long can be difficult for AI systems to process.</li>



<li><strong>Choose your words carefully:</strong> The most effective prompts use clear, direct language. Avoid ambiguity, colorful language, metaphors and slang.</li>



<li><strong>Ask open-ended questions:</strong> Open-ended questions provide more flexibility in the output. For example, a prompt asking to describe complex factors is more likely to elicit a detailed and comprehensive response.</li>



<li><strong>Include context:</strong> Well-designed prompts often include context that helps the AI ​​system tailor its output to the user&#8217;s intended audience.</li>



<li><strong>Set output length goals or limits:</strong> Although AI is designed to be creative, it&#8217;s often a good idea to include guardrails on factors like output length.</li>



<li><strong>Avoid contradictory terms:</strong> Long, complex prompts may include ambiguous or contradictory terms. Make sure all terms are consistent.</li>



<li><strong>Use punctuation to clarify complex prompts:</strong> Just as humans rely on punctuation to help interpret text, AI prompts can also benefit from the judicious use of commas, quotation marks, and line breaks.</li>
</ol>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Understand_the_relevance_of_context"></span>Understand the relevance of context<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>An essential principle of <strong>prompt engineering</strong> is the understanding of the context in which a request is made. Just like in a human conversation, context strongly influences the meaning and relevance of responses. This implies that prompts must be designed in a way that takes into account the specific environment, the user&#8217;s goals and the precise domain of application.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Clarify_user_intent"></span>Clarify user intent<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Clarity of intent in a prompt is crucial to getting a relevant response from the AI. It is important that the prompt is as precise as possible to minimize ambiguity. Sometimes this means rephrasing or adding details that guide the AI ​​toward a more accurate understanding of what the user is looking for.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Structuring_and_prioritizing_information"></span>Structuring and prioritizing information<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>The way a prompt is structured can have a huge impact on the quality of the response you get. This involves prioritizing the information in a logical and coherent way so that the AI ​​can process the request efficiently, and structuring the request so that the most important elements are highlighted, thus guiding the AI ​​towards an adequate response.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Choice_of_language_and_wording"></span>Choice of language and wording<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>The words chosen, the language style and the overall wording of the prompt play a significant role in prompt engineering. Clear, precise language adapted to the AI ​​model in question is essential. For example, some models are more responsive to natural language, while others require more formal or technical formulation.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Iterative_use_and_continuous_improvement"></span>Iterative use and continuous improvement<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>THE <strong>prompt engineering</strong> is often an iterative process. It is not uncommon to have to adjust the prompts several times before arriving at the desired response. Analyzing AI responses and refining prompts based on those responses is an essential part of the prompt engineering process.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Management_of_atypical_results"></span>Management of atypical results<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>It is essential to know how to handle unexpected or atypical results, which can occur even with a well-designed prompt. This includes the ability to diagnose the reasons for such results and rephrase prompts to correct the problem.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Knowledge_of_the_AI_%E2%80%8B%E2%80%8Bmodel_used"></span>Knowledge of the AI ​​model used<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Finally, a thorough understanding of the AI ​​model one is working with is fundamental. Knowing its strengths, limitations, and how it processes prompts is crucial to formulating prompts that will be effectively interpreted and executed by the AI.</p>



<p>You will therefore understand that the <strong>prompt engineering</strong> is an increasingly important skill as AI technologies become more sophisticated and integrated into our daily lives. So start putting it in place as quickly as possible.</p>
