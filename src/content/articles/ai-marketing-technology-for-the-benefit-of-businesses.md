<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="The_marketing_revolution_with_AI"></span>The marketing revolution with AI<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>AI is fueling a revolution in marketing. By leveraging advanced technologies such as machine learning (ML) and non-fungible tokens (NFTs), marketing becomes more effective, more personalized and more consumer-centric.
