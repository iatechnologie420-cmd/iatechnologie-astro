<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="The_evolution_of_artificial_intelligence_from_GPT-3_to_GPT-5"></span>The evolution of artificial intelligence: from GPT-3 to GPT-5<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<p>The meteoric progression of<strong>generative artificial intelligence</strong> in the technological landscape is being driven at this very moment by the phenomenon <strong>ChatGPT</strong>. Starting with GPT-3 then, while waiting for GPT-4, an intermediate version GPT-3.5 was developed by <strong>OpenAI</strong>. But the real revolution is brewing with the anticipation of the future GPT-5, promised to be more intelligent than anything that has existed until now.</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="ChatGPT_An_advanced_conversational_agent"></span>ChatGPT: An advanced conversational agent<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p><strong>ChatGPT</strong>, today it is a generative AI which goes far beyond its status as a tool. Much more than a simple conversational agent, it even passes “theory of mind” tests, thus demonstrating its ability to understand and respond in a relevant and contextualized way.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="GPT-5_The_Future_of_Artificial_Intelligence"></span>GPT-5: The Future of Artificial Intelligence<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Excitement grew around the advent of <strong>GPT-5</strong>. Rumors about its release date, its new features, its price are all indicators of its expected impact. It will be a giant leap in the field of AI, as envisioned by the fathers of ChatGPT: “GPT-5 will be smarter than anything else out there.”</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Threats_and_Challenges_of_Generative_AI"></span>Threats and Challenges of Generative AI<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>But this spectacular progress is not without giving rise to serious concerns. Elon Musk, one of the pioneers of modern technology, is even calling for a six-month pause in research into artificial intelligence, which he describes as a threat to our societies. Many professions seem threatened by this rise in power: taxi drivers, accountants, customer advisors, so many professions on the brink of this upheaval.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Environmental_impact"></span>Environmental impact<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Beyond the societal threat, the digital carbon footprint is exploding because of generative AI. The price of these technological advances could prove too heavy for our planet.</p>

<p>The evolution of artificial intelligence from GPT-3 to GPT-5 perfectly illustrates the speed at which this technology is progressing. Between advances and challenges, the future of AI is an exciting subject, because it will undoubtedly shape our world of tomorrow. So let&#8217;s prepare and anticipate these changes to make the most of this revolution in progress.</p>
