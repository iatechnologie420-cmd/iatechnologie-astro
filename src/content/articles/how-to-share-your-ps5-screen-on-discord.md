<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Step_1_Prepare_your_configuration"></span>Step 1: Prepare your configuration<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<p>When playing games on your PS5, it might be nice to share your experience with your friends on Discord. To do this, you can stream your PS5 screen directly to Discord. In this article, we will explain how to share PS5 screen on Discord in two different methods. Let’s go !</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Check_your_hardware"></span>Check your hardware<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Before you begin, make sure you have the necessary materials. Here’s what you’ll need:<br>– A functional PS5: Make sure your console is in good working order and connected to your TV.<br>– A Discord account: If you don’t have an account yet, go to their website to register for free.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Install_Discord_software_on_your_PC"></span>Install Discord software on your PC<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>To be able to stream your PS5 screen to Discord, you will need to install the Discord app on your PC. Here’s how to do it:<br>1. Open your web browser and go to the Discord website.<br>2. Click the “Download” button to download the application.<br>3. Once the download is complete, open the installer file and follow the on-screen instructions to install the application on your PC.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Configure_your_PS5_settings"></span>Configure your PS5 settings<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Now that you have Discord installed on your PC, it’s time to configure your PS5 settings for streaming to Discord. Here are the steps to follow:</p>



<p>1. Turn on your PS5 and log in to your account.<br>2. Go to the console settings by selecting the gear icon in the upper right corner of the home screen.<br>3. In the settings, select “Capture and broadcast” then “Broadcast settings”.<br>4. In the broadcast settings, activate the “Allow broadcast” option.<br>5. Set the streaming quality according to your preferences. Remember that better quality will require a faster Internet connection.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Connect_your_PS5_to_Discord"></span>Connect your PS5 to Discord<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Now that everything is ready, you can connect your PS5 to Discord to start screen casting. Here’s how to do it:</p>



<p>1. Open Discord on your PC.<br>2. In the Discord app, click “User Settings” at the bottom left.<br>3. In user settings, select “Games” then “Game Activity”.<br>4. Under “Add a game”, click the “+” button. Find and select the “PlayStation” app.<br>5. Once you’ve added the PlayStation app, make sure streaming is enabled by checking that the switch is in the “On” position.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Start_streaming"></span>Start streaming<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Now that everything is set up, you’re ready to start streaming your PS5 screen to Discord. Here are the final steps:</p>



<p>1. Return to your PS5 home screen.<br>2. Play the game you want to share with your friends on Discord.<br>3. In the Discord app on your PC, create a voice room or join one of your friends.<br>4. Click the “Shared Screen” icon in the Voice Room to start streaming.<br>5. Select the “PlayStation” option to stream your PS5 screen.<br>6. Your friends will be able to see your screen and share the gaming experience with you.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="2nd_method_to_connect_your_PS5_to_Discord"></span>2nd method to connect your PS5 to Discord<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Check_your_Internet_connection"></span>Check your Internet connection<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Before connecting your PS5 to Discord, make sure you have a stable internet connection. This will ensure smooth and trouble-free streaming. If you’re using Wi-Fi, make sure you’re close to your router or using a wired connection for optimal performance.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Create_a_Discord_account_and_join_a_server"></span>Create a Discord account and join a server<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>If you don’t already have a Discord account, you’ll need to create one. Visit the official Discord website or download the app to your computer or smartphone. Once you’ve created your account and logged in, join a server using an invite code or by searching for a specific server in Discord settings. Make sure you join a server where you want to share your gaming sessions.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Download_and_install_the_PS_Remote_Play_app"></span>Download and install the PS Remote Play app<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>To stream your PS5 screen to Discord, you will need to use the PS Remote Play app. This free application allows you to stream your PS5 to your computer or smartphone. Visit the official PlayStation website to download the app and follow the installation instructions. Also make sure you have the latest version of your PS5’s system software for optimal compatibility.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Set_up_your_PS5_and_the_PS_Remote_Play_app"></span>Set up your PS5 and the PS Remote Play app<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Once the PS Remote Play app is installed, you will need to configure it to connect your PS5 to Discord. Open the app and follow the on-screen instructions to sign in to your PlayStation Network (PSN) account. Make sure your PS5 is turned on and connected to the same Wi-Fi network as your device where you installed PS Remote Play.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Open_Discord_and_configure_the_streaming_option"></span>Open Discord and configure the streaming option<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Now that you’ve connected your PS5 to the PS Remote Play app, it’s time to set up Discord to stream your screen. Open Discord on your computer or smartphone and make sure you’re signed in to your account. Go to Discord Settings by clicking the gear icon.</p>



<p>In Settings, go to the “Voice & Video” tab and scroll down to the “Detected Devices” section. Under “Video Device” you should see “PS Remote Play.” Select this option so Discord can use your PS Remote Play app as a streaming source.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Start_streaming_your_PS5_screen_on_Discord"></span>Start streaming your PS5 screen on Discord<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Now that everything is set up, it’s time to start streaming your PS5 screen to Discord. Return to the PS Remote Play app on your computer or smartphone, and launch a game on your PS5. You will be able to see your game displayed on the PS Remote Play app.<br>Return to Discord and join the server where you want to share your gaming session. Click the video camera icon in the voice channel to start streaming. Your friends will now be able to see your PS5 screen live on Discord, and you can all chat while playing together.</p>



<p><strong>Trick :</strong> Make sure your PS5’s privacy settings allow content to be streamed to other devices. You can check these settings in your PS5 settings, under the “Captures & Streaming” tab.</p>



<p>Now that you know how to connect your PS5 to Discord, you can enjoy shared gaming sessions with your friends and community. Remember to follow Discord’s rules and ask permission before streaming someone else’s game. Have fun !</p>
