<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="The_phenomenon_of_%E2%80%9CNPC_controllers%E2%80%9D_on_TikTok"></span>The phenomenon of “NPC controllers” on TikTok<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<p>      In the infinite universe of <strong>TikTok</strong>, a rather singular phenomenon has captured the attention of users: “ <strong>NPC controllers</strong> » (Non-Player Characters). Previously confined to video games, the expression &#8220;NPC&#8221; is now creeping into social media jargon, defining an experience that mixes augmented reality and social interaction. But what is really behind this craze for controlling NPCs on <strong>TikTok</strong>, and why does it arouse both fascination and debate?</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="The_concept_of_NPC_on_Tiktok"></span>The concept of NPC on Tiktok<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>      Originally, an NPC is a character controlled by the program of a video game, which serves to provide interaction, quests or services to the player. When we transpose this concept into the social sphere of <strong>TikTok</strong>, an &#8220;NPC controller&#8221; refers to a person who engages in suggestive behavior that invites others to react in a certain way, as if they were scripted or predictable, like game characters.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="The_birth_of_a_viral_trend%E2%80%A6strange"></span>The birth of a viral trend&#8230;strange <span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>      This trend of NPCs on TikTok grew, content creators began to share videos where they programmed certain types of reactions, often suggestive, provocative, in exchange for donations and gifts on their TikTok lives. By influencing spectators with subtle gestures or keywords.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Psychology_and_manipulation_Innocent_play_or_dubious_practice"></span>Psychology and manipulation: Innocent play or dubious practice?<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>      The most controversial aspect of the phenomenon involves the potential manipulation of unsuspecting people. Although some videos remain clearly harmless and humorous, this trend raises ethical questions about the line between entertainment and respect for the will of others. Don&#8217;t they treat their spectators like wallets on legs who are there only to spit out a maximum, all while exploiting emotional social poverty?</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="A_fascination_with_social_control"></span>A fascination with social control<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>      This phenomenon reflects a fascination with social influence and control. The &#8220;NPC controllers&#8221; on <strong>TikTok</strong> reignite the debate about the impact of social media on our free will and the way in which we can be &#8220;programmed&#8221; by trends and mass behaviors.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Behaving_like_an_NPC_the_consequences"></span>Behaving like an NPC: the consequences <span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" width="1792" height="1024" src="/images/blog/Elles-se-laissent-controler-comme-des-PNJ-sur-TikTok-et-ca-rapporte-gros-1.png" alt="" class="wp-image-1575" srcset="/images/blog/Elles-se-laissent-controler-comme-des-PNJ-sur-TikTok-et-ca-rapporte-gros-1.png 1792w, /images/blog/Elles-se-laissent-controler-comme-des-PNJ-sur-TikTok-et-ca-rapporte-gros-1-300x171.png 300w, /images/blog/Elles-se-laissent-controler-comme-des-PNJ-sur-TikTok-et-ca-rapporte-gros-1-1024x585.png 1024w, /images/blog/Elles-se-laissent-controler-comme-des-PNJ-sur-TikTok-et-ca-rapporte-gros-1-150x86.png 150w, /images/blog/Elles-se-laissent-controler-comme-des-PNJ-sur-TikTok-et-ca-rapporte-gros-1-768x439.png 768w, /images/blog/Elles-se-laissent-controler-comme-des-PNJ-sur-TikTok-et-ca-rapporte-gros-1-1536x878.png 1536w" sizes="(max-width: 1792px) 100vw, 1792px" /></figure>



<p>In the world of video games, an NPC (Non-Player Character) is a character controlled by the system, acting according to predefined scripts, without free will or consciousness. But what happens when this notion extends beyond screens, into everyday human behavior?</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="What_does_it_mean_to_behave_like_an_NPC"></span>What does it mean to behave like an NPC?<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>Behave like one <strong>NPC</strong> involves following daily and social routines without adding critical thought or personal touch. This may suggest restricted individualism, where decisions and actions are heavily influenced, or even dictated, by conventions or external authorities. The comparison with a <strong>NPC</strong> highlights a lack of authenticity or originality in a person&#8217;s behavior.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Psychological_impact_of_adopting_NPC_behaviors"></span>Psychological impact of adopting NPC behaviors<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Psychologically, living like a <strong>NPC</strong> can lead to feelings of alienation and a lack of personal commitment. Repetition of routines without active engagement can decrease mental stimulation and increase feelings of emptiness and dissatisfaction. There is also a risk of weakening social and self-reflection skills, skills essential for personal development.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Social_consequences_of_NPC_behavior"></span>Social consequences of NPC behavior<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>On a social level, this type of behavior can result in superficial relationship dynamics. Lack of initiative or self-expression hinders the creation of deep, meaningful relationships, and this can lead to social isolation. Furthermore, strictly conforming to social expectations without questioning can perpetuate harmful norms and stereotypes.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="How_to_escape_the_NPC_mentality"></span>How to escape the NPC mentality<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>The escape from the mentality of <strong>NPC</strong> is autonomy, creativity and critical thinking. Engaging in activities that nurture critical thinking, empathy, and self-expression is vital. Practices such as meditation, the arts, writing, or even civic engagement, encourage individuals to be fully active in their lives and to enrich their social experience.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Role_of_media_and_technology"></span>Role of media and technology<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Modern media and technologies, such as social networks, often amplify behavior of <strong>NPC</strong>. They emphasize uniform patterns of success and superficial interactions, which can encourage conformity without introspection. However, they can also serve as platforms for awareness and personal development, provided they are used thoughtfully and critically.</p>



<p>Adopt behavioral traits of <strong>NPC</strong> in real life can have profound negative impacts on mental health and social relationships. Recognizing and moving away from these patterns requires introspection and boldness to build an authentic and fulfilling life journey. It is essential to remain aware of these dynamics to live fully and enrich the communities in which we operate.</p>
