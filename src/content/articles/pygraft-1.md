<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="PyGraft_%D8%AF_%D8%AE%D9%84%D8%A7%D8%B5%DB%90_%D8%B3%D8%B1%DA%86%DB%8C%D9%86%DB%90_%DA%89%DB%8C%D9%BC%D8%A7_%D9%88%DB%8C%D8%B2_%D9%86%D9%88%DB%8C_%D8%B3%D8%AA%D9%88%D8%B1%DB%8C"></span>PyGraft: د خلاصې سرچینې ډیټا ویز نوی ستوری<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>
