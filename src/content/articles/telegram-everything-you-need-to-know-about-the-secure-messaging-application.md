<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="What_is_Telegram"></span>What is Telegram?<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<p>    <strong>Telegram</strong> is an instant messaging application focused on speed and security. It has become one of the most popular communications platforms in the world thanks to its combination of advanced features, intuitive interface and commitment to protecting user privacy. </p>



<p>Since its launch in 2013, <strong>Telegram</strong> has stood out among a multitude of messaging applications, positioning itself as an alternative communication tool that respects and values ​​confidentiality and freedom of expression.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="The_origins_of_Telegram"></span>The origins of Telegram<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>    The story of <strong>Telegram</strong> begins with two brothers, Nikolai and Pavel Durov, who previously founded the very popular Russian social network VKontakte. Concerned by the evolution of online privacy and encouraged by their desire to create a secure communication platform, they launched <strong>Telegram</strong> in August 2013. Their goal was clear: to offer a service that promotes freedom and is inaccessible to surveillance agencies and hackers.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="The_Philosophy_of_Telegram"></span>The Philosophy of Telegram<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>    At the heart of the philosophy of <strong>Telegram</strong> lies the belief that secure communication is a universal right. The platform is built on strong cryptography principles, allowing users to exchange messages without fear of illegitimate interceptions. With features like secret chats, end-to-end encryption, and automatic message destruction, <strong>Telegram</strong> makes a point of guaranteeing the confidentiality of its users. </p>



<p>Additionally, the app is free and stands firm on its promise to never sell ads or user data, a stance that defines its philosophy of respecting privacy and user experience above all else.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="End-to-end_encryption"></span>End-to-end encryption<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>A crucial point of safety <strong>Telegram</strong> is its end-to-end encryption system. Concretely, this means that sent messages are transformed into a secret code on the sender&#8217;s device and are only decrypted on the recipient&#8217;s device. This prevents unauthorized access during transmission, ensuring confidentiality of communications.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Secret_conversations"></span>Secret conversations<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Beyond classic encryption, <strong>Telegram</strong> offers the <strong>“Secret Conversations”</strong>. These include additional features such as message self-destruction and are not stored on servers. <strong>Telegram</strong>, thus providing an increased level of security for the most sensitive information.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Respect_for_privacy"></span>Respect for privacy<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p><strong>Telegram</strong> also stands out for its respect for privacy. Users can hide their phone number and control who can see them, reducing the risk of spam and harassment. Public groups and channels allow the dissemination of information without exposing the identity of participants.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Telegram_bots"></span>Telegram bots<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>THE <strong>bots</strong> on <strong>Telegram</strong> are third-party applications that work inside messaging. They can accomplish a multitude of tasks, from handling administrative tasks to playing interactive games. These bots open up a world of possibilities to automate and enrich the user experience.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Intuitive_user_interface"></span>Intuitive user interface<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>The interface of <strong>Telegram</strong>, known for its simplicity and ease of use, allows users to easily navigate between messages, groups and channels. The application also offers extensive customization options, for visual comfort and a tailor-made experience.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Sharing_large_files"></span>Sharing large files<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>Another remarkable feature of <strong>Telegram</strong> is its ability to handle heavy file sharing. Unlike many of its competitors, <strong>Telegram</strong> allows users to send files up to 2GB, making sharing videos, heavy projects, or large photo collections a breeze.</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Constant_updates"></span>Constant updates<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p><strong>Telegram</strong> stands out for its constant evolution. The app is regularly updated with new features and security improvements to best meet the changing needs of its users.</p>
